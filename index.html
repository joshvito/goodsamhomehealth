Caring and Compassion

Good Samaritan is a home care agency licensed by the New York State Department of Health, specializing in helping Seniors. We provide a variety of services that help Seniors with their Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) so they may maintain their independence and dignity while remaining in their own homes for as long as possible. Veterans Programs

Many of our Veterans and their surviving spouses are eligible for the VA Aid and Attendance benefit. This is a federally funded benefit for veterans who served in World War II, December 7, 1941 – December 31, 1046, Korean War, June 27, 1950 – January 31, 1955, Vietnam War, August 5, 1964 – May 7, 1975 and Persian Gulf War, August 2, 1990.Learn more
